    Privacy Policy

    Effective Date: June 1, 2025
    Last Updated: June 1, 2025

    At Buoy, your privacy is paramount. We’re committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring transparency about how we handle information. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can manage your data.

    1. Information We Collect

    Non-Personal Information

    We collect anonymous data (e.g., browser type, device info, referral pages) for analytics, performance, and site optimization. This data cannot identify you.

    Personal Information

    We only collect personal information (e.g., email address) when you choose to provide it—such as subscribing to updates or contacting us.

    2. Use of Cookies and Analytics

    We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance functionality, remember your preferences, and analyze traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

    Third-Party Tools:
    We may use privacy-focused analytics tools (e.g., Plausible or Fathom) to avoid invasive tracking. No data is sold or shared for advertising purposes.

    3. How We Use Your Information

    • Provide and improve our services
    • Communicate with you (only if you opt-in)
    • Secure the site and prevent abuse

    We do not sell your information. Ever.

    4. Third-Party Access

    We may work with trusted service providers (e.g., email delivery or analytics) under strict confidentiality agreements. They are only allowed to use your information to perform the specific services we request.

    5. Data Security

    We use industry-standard measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

    6. Your Rights

    If you have any concerns about your personal data, feel free to contact us, and we’ll do our best to assist.

    7. Children’s Privacy

    We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe this has happened, please contact us.

    8. External Links

    Buoy may link to third-party sites. We’re not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

    9. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be posted here and announced on our homepage. Continued use of Buoy means you agree to the updated terms.
